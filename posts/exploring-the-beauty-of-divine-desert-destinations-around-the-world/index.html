Exploring Desert Destinations Around the World

Published: 2024

Deserts cover about one-third of the Earth's land surface, each offering unique ecosystems, cultures, and breathtaking beauty.

Namib Desert, Namibia

Home to the world's tallest sand dunes at Sossusvlei, the Namib is one of the oldest deserts on Earth. The contrast of red dunes against blue sky is photographer's paradise.

Thar Desert, India

The Great Indian Desert offers vibrant culture alongside stunning landscapes. Jaisalmer's golden fort rises from the desert like a mirage, and camel safaris reveal the desert's hidden beauty.

White Sands, New Mexico

A surreal landscape of brilliant white gypsum dunes in southern New Mexico. The dunes shift constantly, creating an ever-changing landscape that glows at sunset.

Desert Travel Tips

  • Carry plenty of water and sun protection
  • Visit during cooler months when possible
  • Respect local cultures and environments
  • Book experienced guides for remote areas
