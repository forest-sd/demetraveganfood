Best Desert Destinations for Your Next Adventure

Published: 2024

The world's deserts offer some of the most spectacular landscapes and unique experiences for adventurous travelers.

Sahara Desert, Morocco

Experience the magic of camel treks, Berber camps, and star-filled nights in the world's largest hot desert. The golden dunes of Erg Chebbi are unforgettable.

Atacama Desert, Chile

The driest desert on Earth offers geysers, salt flats, and some of the world's best stargazing. The Valley of the Moon is a surreal landscape of eroded salt formations.

Wadi Rum, Jordan

This dramatic red desert was the backdrop for Lawrence of Arabia. Experience Bedouin hospitality, rock climbing, and camping under vast desert skies.

Joshua Tree, California

Just a few hours from Los Angeles, Joshua Tree offers unique rock formations, otherworldly trees, and excellent hiking trails in a stunning desert environment.
